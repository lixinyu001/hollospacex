About Ocean Guardians

Since 2012, we've been dedicated to marine conservation through scientific research, community action, and policy advocacy.

Our Mission

Ocean Guardians is a non-profit organization dedicated to protecting marine ecosystems through scientific research, community engagement, and policy advocacy to reduce ocean pollution and preserve marine biodiversity.

Our Conservation Initiatives

Marine Ecology Biodiversity
